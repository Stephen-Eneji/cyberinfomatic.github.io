    HOW DO I STAY CONNECTED ON LINKEDIN

    Linked in, like most connecting organizations and social media is for interconnection and most importantly, exposure. To get the most exposure on linkedIn, developers should create a network, post regularly and request for interviews on the necessary groups and FinTech organizations that are up for hire. To get the most out of LinkedIn, set up a professional account, upload completed projects and resume, formal information, professional experiences and skills.
