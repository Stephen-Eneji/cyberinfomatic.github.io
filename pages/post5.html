    User Interface/User Experience (UI/UX)

    Infomation
    1st-June-22 | 11:12am
    Stephen Eneji


    In simple terms, a User Interface (UI) is the features of a device or an application that allow a user to interact with it. It makes it easier for your target audience to interact with it. 

                            
                            Hello World ðŸ™‚

    UI design includes many elements and even the smallest changes can have a huge impact on its quality. Things that might sound silly such as the shape or size of a button on a page can determine if the user can find it easily or not. A Good UI design is inclusive of an organization color pattern, Logo, Website design, mobile application design and desktop preview and interactivity.


    RELATIONSHIP BETWEEN UI/UX

    UI is the design, UX is the measurement of the product relevance experience from users. User interface design (UI) is directly related to user experience (UX), they work hand in hand for any product.
    Ultimately, having a good UI/UX is the most important and first phase of any long term product or service.
